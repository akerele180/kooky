THESTORY

After a terrible and devastating wildfire,
the homes of thousands of native Australian wildlife were destroyed.
With no homes to return to, the kangaroos that fled spread out across the globe.
And thus, the kooky kangaroos were born.
After adapting to their environments the kangaroos picked up on human behaviors and even began to wear human clothes! The kangaroos still want to help their friends back home so they will be donating to the WIRES Australia foundation to save and rehabilitate thousands of other animals.
